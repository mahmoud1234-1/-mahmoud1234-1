About the project

Why Focus Up exists

Many students do not need harsher restrictions. They need a smarter, more supportive system that helps them stay focused during study time and reduce late-night scrolling without feeling controlled.

Focus Up was designed as a hybrid solution that combines motivation-first rewards, optional friction, progress visibility, and sleep protection in one youth-centered experience.

1

Notice the pattern

Students recognize how late-night scrolling and constant checking affect sleep, stress, and grades.

2

Start small

Short sessions and beginner-friendly challenges reduce resistance and make progress feel possible.

3

Build habits

Rewards, streaks, and weekly reports turn focus into a routine that feels achievable and positive.

Core features

Everything students need to regain control

⏱️

Focus Sessions

Start short Pomodoro-style sessions with flexible timing that helps students study without feeling trapped.

🌙

Sleep Shield

Gentle bedtime support that reduces late-night scrolling through wind-down prompts and optional restrictions.

🏆

Rewards & Streaks

Earn points, badges, levels, and streaks that make discipline feel motivating instead of punishing.

📈

Progress Analytics

See weekly progress, focus time, and habit consistency in a simple privacy-first dashboard.

🎯

Challenges

Join optional friend challenges and exam-week packs designed for student life.

🔒

Student-First Privacy

Opt-in tracking, transparent permissions, and a supportive experience without shame-based pressure.
